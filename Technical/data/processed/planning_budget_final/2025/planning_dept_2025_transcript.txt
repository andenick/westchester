Department Of Planning (19)
The Department of Planning, as staff to the County Planning Board, must prepare an annual report with recommendations on capital projects
proposed to be undertaken by County departments and prepares reports on land use matters referred to the County Planning Board by
municipalities. Also, as staff to the County Planning Board, the Department reviews certain land use applications that may impact County or
State properties. The Department of Planning oversees the preparation of all environmental reviews for County actions including legislation,
real estate transactions, capital projects, and building and site improvements in compliance with the New York State Environmental Quality
Review Act (SEQRA) and National Environmental Protection Act (NEPA). The Department of Planning represents the County in federally
mandated transportation planning activities in order to maintain the County's eligibility for federal transit and federal highway funds, in addition
to supporting system ridership and demographic data and analysis. The Department of Planning performs these activities reviews under the
following New York State Laws and State Codes, Rules and Regulations:
