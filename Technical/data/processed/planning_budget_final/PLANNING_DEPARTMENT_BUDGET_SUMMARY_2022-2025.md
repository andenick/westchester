## Executive Summary

The Westchester County Planning Department budget has decreased significantly from 2022 to 2025:
- **2022 Total Expenditures:** $21,524,501

## Department Mission & Responsibilities

### Mission Statement
The mission of the Department of Planning is to shape and influence growth and development in Westchester County in order to improve quality of life and protect the environment, resulting in more livable, sustainable and resilient communities.

### Key Responsibilities

#### General Government Support
- Formulate and recommend major land use & development plans
- Planning and oversight of County's Five Year Capital Improvement Plan
- Environmental reviews (SEQRA/NEPA compliance)
- Staff support to County Planning Board

#### Community & Economic Development
- Strengthen downtowns and promote transit-oriented development
- Administer affordable housing programs
- Lead Westchester 2025 regional planning initiatives
- Eviction and foreclosure prevention programs

#### Environmental
- Water quality and natural resource protection
- Flooding mitigation and hazard management
- Stormwater management and MS4 compliance
- Historic resource protection
- Open Space Program and RiverWalk projects

#### Transportation
- Bee-Line System planning and oversight
- Federal/state transportation grant applications
- Transit system performance monitoring
- Coordinate transportation planning with NY Metropolitan Transportation Council (NYMTC)

#### Health & Public Safety
- Preserve existing affordable housing stock
- Lead-based paint hazard remediation
- Food security and social equity initiatives
- Hazard mitigation planning
