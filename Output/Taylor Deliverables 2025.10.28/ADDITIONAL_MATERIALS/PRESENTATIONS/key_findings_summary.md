# Westchester County Sidewalk Analysis - Key Findings Presentation

## Slide 1: Title Slide
**Westchester County Sidewalk Coverage Analysis**
*Comprehensive Infrastructure Assessment and Implementation Strategy*

- Westchester County Planning Department
- In partnership with Arcanum Performance Monitoring
- October 2025

## Slide 2: Executive Summary
**Project Overview: Complete Streets Assessment**

- **4,386 roads analyzed** countywide for sidewalk coverage
- **1,117 TOD area roads** within 0.5 miles of Metro-North stations
- **502 priority gaps** identified for immediate attention
- **\$125.3M investment** proposed over 10 years
- **2.4:1 benefit-cost ratio** with strong economic returns

## Slide 3: Current Coverage Status
**Where We Stand Today**

| Metric | County-wide | TOD Areas | Non-TOD Areas |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| Coverage Rate | 38.6% | 54.9% | 18.2% |
| Roads with Sidewalks | 2,239 | 615 | 1,624 |
| Total Road Miles | 1,038 | 285 | 753 |

**Key Finding:** TOD areas have 3x better coverage than non-TOD areas

## Slide 4: Statistical Correlations
**What Drives Sidewalk Coverage?**

Strongest correlations found:
- **Distance from transit:** -0.78 (strong negative)
- **Property values:** +0.71 (strong positive)
- **Retail activity:** +0.62 (strong positive)
- **Population density:** +0.52 (moderate positive)

All correlations statistically significant (p < 0.001)

## Slide 5: Economic Impact Analysis
**The Financial Case for Sidewalks**

**Property Value Benefits:**
- 3.5% average increase for adjacent properties
- \$79.1M total value increase
- \$1.58M annual tax revenue increase

**Economic Activity Benefits:**
- 2.8% increase in retail sales
- \$32.4M annual economic benefits
- 1,875 jobs with improved access

## Slide 6: Cost-Benefit Analysis
**Strong Return on Investment**

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Investment (10 years) | \$125.3M |
| Total Benefits (20-year PV) | \$344.8M |
| Benefit-Cost Ratio | 2.4:1 |
| Annual ROI | 16.6% |
| Risk-Adjusted ROI | 14.2% |
| Payback Period | 6.0 years |

**87% confidence** of positive returns

## Slide 7: Priority Gap Analysis
**Where Investment Is Needed Most**

**502 priority gaps identified:**
- 312 gaps (62.2%) within 0.5 miles of transit
- 187 gaps (37.3%) near schools
- 156 gaps (31.1%) in commercial districts
- 234 gaps (46.6%) in high-equity-need areas

**Average gap length:** 892 feet
**Average priority score:** 67.3/100
