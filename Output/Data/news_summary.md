# Westchester County News Summary

## Top Stories:
*   Westchester County Executive Ken Jenkins has applauded a federal court decision regarding Indian Point, and the county is challenging plans that could lead to radioactive wastewater discharge into the Hudson River.
*   A new law in Westchester mandates first responders carry EpiPens, and the County Executive signed "Gio's Law" to equip county police officers with them.
*   Westchester County Airport's renewable diesel trial has shown cost savings, leading to a full transition for its airport fleet.
