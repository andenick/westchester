
# Westchester County Demographics Summary

## Population and Age:
*   The median age in Westchester County is around 41.5 to 42 years.
*   The age distribution in 2023 shows approximately 17.5% under 15, 12.6% aged 15-24, 24.6% aged 25-44, 27.7% aged 45-64, and 17.7% aged 65 or older.
*   Between 2008-2012 and 2018-2022, the population aged 85 or older increased by 21%, and those aged 60 to 84 increased by 26%, indicating an aging population trend.

## Racial and Ethnic Composition:
*   According to the 2020 census, the racial makeup was 57.4% non-Hispanic White, 15.6% African American, 0.4% Native American, 5.4% Asian, 0.1% Pacific Islander, 7.6% from other races, and 3.2% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino individuals of any race constituted 21.8% of the population.
*   More recent data from 2023 indicates the largest ethnic groups are White (Non-Hispanic) at 50%, Black or African American (Non-Hispanic) at 12.9%, Other (Hispanic) at 12.3%, Two Races Including Other (Hispanic) at 7.29%, and Asian (Non-Hispanic) at 6.01%.
*   The Hispanic/Latino population has shown significant growth, increasing by 52,648 between 2010 and 2022, making up 26.8% of the population in 2020.

## Income:
*   The median household income in Westchester County was $118,411 in 2023, showing a 3.28% increase from $114,651 in 2022. Other sources report a median income of $145,587 in 2023.
*   The average annual household income in 2023 was $217,133.
*   In 2021, the county's median household income had risen to $105,387.
*   Households led by individuals aged 25 to 44 had a median income of $173,426, while those aged 45 to 64 earned $169,444. Younger households (under 25) had a median income of $33,750, and those over 65 had about $95,290.
*   Median incomes in 2018-2022 were highest among Asian ($162,400) and White ($135,500) residents, and lowest among Hispanic ($83,200) and African American ($76,200) residents.

## Language and Citizenship:
*   In 2023, 89.8% of Westchester County residents were US citizens, and 25.4% were born outside the country.
*   In 2020, 71.7% spoke only English at home, while 14.4% spoke Spanish, 3.5% Italian, 1.1% Portuguese, and 1.1% French.

## Households:
*   Westchester County has 370,554 households, with an average of 2 members each. Of these, 68.2% are families.
*   In 2020, there were 337,142 households, with 34% having children under 18, 53.9% being married couples, and 30.2% being non-families.
